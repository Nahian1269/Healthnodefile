# **App Name**: HealthNode

## Core Features:

- AI Chatbot: AI-driven tool to provide personalized health and wellness advice, answer questions, and offer support.
- Meal Planner: Suggest personalized meal plans based on user preferences, dietary restrictions, and fitness goals.
- BMI/BMR Calculator: Calculate and display Body Mass Index (BMI) and Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR).
- Fitness Assessment: Enable users to track fitness metrics and progress using health reports and minimal dashboards
- Freehand Exercise List: Provide a searchable list of freehand exercises and their descriptions.
- Gym Trainer Portal: Allow certified gym trainers to create profiles and offer consultation services to users.
- Supplement/Equipment Store: Allow users to purchase supplements and gym equipment.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep teal (#468499) to evoke a sense of health, nature, and balance.
- Background color: Very light blue (#E0F4F7), almost white, to keep the site bright, airy, and calm.
- Accent color: Warm orange (#E6772E) for key CTAs, to draw attention and convey energy.
- Body text: 'PT Sans', sans-serif
- Headline Font: 'Playfair', serif. Pair with PT Sans.
- Use clean and modern icons related to health and fitness for easy navigation.
- Ensure a clean and intuitive layout to facilitate easy navigation.
- Subtle animations to provide feedback and enhance user engagement.
